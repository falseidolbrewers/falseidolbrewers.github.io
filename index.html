Meet Our Brewers

Shawn Steele

Co-owner & Head Brewer

Shawn learned to brew professionally at Akasha Brewing Company, where he was a brewer for roughly two years. His passion continued to grow and he knew he needed to open his own place. He's also very passionate about music of nearly every genre, and particularly enjoys playing piano at home and collecting records. You'll hear his musical influences playing at False Idol.

Chris Woodall

Assistant Brewer & Brewpub Shift Lead

Chris joined the False Idol team in October 2018. He’s been a beer enthusiast and home brewer for more years than he’d care to admit. Learning to brew with Shawn on a larger scale has been a bit of a dream come true for Chris. His mission is to create great beers and help grow the False Idol brand in the process.
